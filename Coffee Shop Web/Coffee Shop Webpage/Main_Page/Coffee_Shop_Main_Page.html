5 HEALTH BENEFITS OF COFFEE

  1. Coffee boosts your physical performance.

    2. Have a cup of black coffee about an hour before workout and your performance can improve by 11-12%. Caffeine increases adrenaline levels in your blood. Adrenaline is your body’s “fight or flight” hormone which helps you to prepare for physical exertion.

  2. Coffee may help you lose weight.

    3. Coffee contains magnesium and potassium, which helps the human body use insulin, regulating blood sugar levels and reducing your craving for sugary treats and snacks.

  3. Coffee helps you burn fat.

    4. Caffeine helps fat cells break down body fat and use it as fuel for training.

  4. Coffee helps you focus and stay alert.

    5. Moderate caffeine intake, 1-6 cups a day, helps you focus and improves your mental alertness.

  5. Coffee lowers risk of death.

    6. Studies have shown that coffee drinker’s overall risk of premature death is 25% lower than of those who don’t drink coffee.
